TIME-AWARE RECOMMENDER SYSTEMS USING DEEP NEURAL NETWORKS

Deep neural networks have achieved state-of-the-art performance for tasks like multi class image classification, speech recognition, and various NLP tasks like part-of-speech (POS) tagging. At the same time, less attention has been paid to applying deep learning to learn recommender systems. One possible reason could be the sparsity of training data. In recent years, a few attempts have been made to apply neural networks to learn recommender systems. Salakhutdinov et al 2007 used Restricted Boltzmann Machines (RBMs).
